Gentlemen,Passy, Oct. 28. 1779.
I send you enclos’d a Bill of Exchange for 100£ sterling, with which please to credit my Account. With great Esteem, I am Gentlemen y. m. o. & m. h. s.

Messrs. Browns and Collinson.
