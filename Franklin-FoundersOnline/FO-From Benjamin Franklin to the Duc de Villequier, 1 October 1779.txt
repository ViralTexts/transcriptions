Sir
Passy, Oct. 1. 1779.
I return herewith the Queries you did me the honour to send me, having written the Answers in the Margin. If they are satisfactory to you it will be a pleasure to me, being with great Respect Sir, Your most obedient and most humble servant
M. Le Duc De Villequier.
