Sir
Passy, Oct. 2. 1779.
Enclosed I Send you the Judgment I have made on the eight Ransomers, the Proces Verbaux being come to hand since my last.
I have also received the Proces verbal relating to  taken by the black Prince of Salem  Captain. I suppose this to be another Vessel. Please to let me know if you have any Concern in it.
Mr. Torris.
