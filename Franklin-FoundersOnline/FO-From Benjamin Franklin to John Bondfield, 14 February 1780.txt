Author: Franklin, Benjamin

Sir,
Passy, Feb. 14. 1780.
I request that you would enquire into the facts represented in the enclos’d Petition, and if you find them true, that you would defray the reasonable Expences of the poor Man’s sickness, and make a Present to his friend of Two Guineas as a small Acknowledgment of his Hospitality and kindness. I have the honour to be, sir, &c.
Mr. Bondfield.
