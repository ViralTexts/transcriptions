Dear Friend
Philada. June 10. 1756
It is now a long time since I had the Pleasure of a Line from you; I am now returnâ€™d from Virginia, where I was near too Months. I should be glad to learn from you the present State of the Forces in your County, and of the People. If in any Thing I can serve you, command freely your old Friend and most obedient humble Servant
B Franklin
Governor Pownall is daily expected at New York, with Lord Loudon.

Major Parsons
