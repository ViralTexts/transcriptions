Sir,
Passy Aug 12. 1779
I received duely the Letter you did me the honour of writing to me of the 6th. Instant, together with the Pacquet of Papers therein mentioned relating to the English Vessel called the Betsey, agreable to the Inventory that accompanied them.
I have the honour to be with great Regard Sir, Your most obedient and most humble Servant
B Franklin
M. Perret
