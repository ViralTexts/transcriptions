Dear Sir,
Philada. Dec. 4. 1753
This serves to enclose second Bills for One Hundred Pound Sterling, sent per Richey. I am, ut supra.
B Franklin
 Addressed: To Mr Wm Strahan  Printer  London  Per Capt. Mitchell.
