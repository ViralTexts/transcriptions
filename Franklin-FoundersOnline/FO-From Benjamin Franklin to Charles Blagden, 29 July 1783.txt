 Title: FO-From Benjamin Franklin to Charles Blagden, 29 July 1783
Date: 1783-07-29
Author: Franklin, Benjamin

            Passy, July 29. 1783—
          
          Dr Franklin presents his Compliments to Dr Blagden, requests his Care
            of the enclos’d, and wishes him a good Journey with a happy Sight of his Friends &
              Country.
