Gentlemen
Feb. 14. 69
Enclos’d is Dr. Rush’s Signature which you desired. I thank you for so readily furnishing the Letter of Credit. Yours &c
B Franklin
 
Addressed: To / Messrs. Smith, Wright & / Gray, / Bankers / Lombard street
