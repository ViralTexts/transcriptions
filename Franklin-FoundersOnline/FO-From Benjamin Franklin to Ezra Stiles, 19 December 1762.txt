Dear Sir,
Philada. Decr. 19. 1762
This Line is just to salute you, and acquaint you with my Return to America, Thanks to God, well and hearty. I hope you are so. With this you will receive a Thermometer which craves your Acceptance. With the greatest Esteem, I am, Dear Sir, Your most obedient and most humble Servant
B Franklin
Revd Mr. Stiles
