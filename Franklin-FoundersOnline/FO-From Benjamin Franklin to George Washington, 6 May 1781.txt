Dear Sir,
Passy, May. 6. 1781

I beg leave to present him to your Exlleny. as a Gentleman of Character, & who has long distinguished himself in England as a firm Friend to the Cause of America. Endorsed: The Honble. Dr. Franklin 6th. May 1781
