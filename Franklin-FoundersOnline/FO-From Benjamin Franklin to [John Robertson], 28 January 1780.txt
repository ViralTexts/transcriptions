Sir
Passy, Jan. 28. 1780.
I received yours of the 17th. Instant, relating to your son. He was here the  and I assisted him with some money, five Louis to help him forward to you. He set out for Nantes I suppose immediately, and I hope he is safe with you before this time. I am, sir &c.
Capt. Robinson, (Send it under Cover to Mr. John Ross Nantes)
