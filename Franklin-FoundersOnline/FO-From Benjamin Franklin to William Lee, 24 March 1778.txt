Passy, March 24. 1778
Dr. Franklin’s Compliments to Mr. W. Lee and informs him, that the Treaty of Alliance, is ordered by the Court of France, to be kept a Secret, ’till they think fit to publish it.
 
Addressed: Honble William Lee Esqr / Chaillot.
