[New York, Saturday, June 4, 1757]
I enclose you an Order of Cousin James’s on Mr. Collins for 35 Dollars. Please to receive them, and send them per first safe hand to my Wife. If you can get it in Gold, it may be enclosed in a little Packet and sent per Post … Billy presents his Duty. … I pray God to preserve you both.
