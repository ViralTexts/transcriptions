SirPassy 1st. Agt. 1780.
The Bearer of this, Mr. appleton, is lately arrived from Boston. He is recommended to me as a young Gentleman of excellent character, & as such, I beg leave to introduce him to your acquaintance and Civilities.
With great Esteem, I am Dear Sir your most obedient & most humble Servant.
B. Franklin.
M. Dumas.
