Sir,Passy, April 10. 1781.
I am much oblig’d by your kind Attention to the Request of Mr. Williams, of Nantes, and beg you to accept my Thanks. I am with great Respect, Sir, Your most obedient & most humble Servant.
M. Necker Comptroller General of the Finances.—
