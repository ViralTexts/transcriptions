Thursday morng.—[1778 or after]
All the American Papers will be sent to Mr Genet this Evening. Mr Franklin sends the enclos’d four, for a Beginning.
 
Addressed: A Monsr / Monsieur Genet / Bureau des Interprêtes / Versailles
