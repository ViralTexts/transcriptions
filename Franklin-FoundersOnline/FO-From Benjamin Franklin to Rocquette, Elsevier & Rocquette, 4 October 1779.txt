Gentlemen,
Passy, Oct. 4. 1779.
I have receiv’d lately two Pacquets thro’ your hands, the last with your favour of the 20th. Past. I am much oblig’d by your Care of them, and have the honour to be, Gentlemen Your most obedient and most humble servt.
Messrs. J Rocquette and Elzevier and Brothers Roquettes Rotterdam.
