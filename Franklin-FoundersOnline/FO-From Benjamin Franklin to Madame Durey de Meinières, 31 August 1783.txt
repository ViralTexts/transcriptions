 Title: FO-From Benjamin Franklin to Madame Durey de Meinières, 31 August 1783
Date: 1783-08-31
Author: Franklin, Benjamin
Page URL: https://founders.archives.gov/documents/Franklin/01-40-02-0348
---

          
            Passy, Augt 31. 1783.—
          
          I send with great Pleasure the Constitutions of America to my dear & much respected Neighbour, being
            happy to have any thing in my Power to give that she will do me the honour to accept,
            and that may be agreable to her. I am, ever, with sincere Esteem, my dear Friend, Yours
            most affectionately
          
            B Franklin
