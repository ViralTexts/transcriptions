Saturday Evening [November or December, 1769]
Dr. Franklin presents his best Respects to Mr. Benzel, and will be glad to see him and his Friend tomorrow morning at Eleven a Clock, if that time will suit them, and they think fit to do him the Honour of a Visit in Craven street.
 Addressed: To / Mr Benzel / at Mrs Philips’s / Jeweller. / St Martin’s Court
