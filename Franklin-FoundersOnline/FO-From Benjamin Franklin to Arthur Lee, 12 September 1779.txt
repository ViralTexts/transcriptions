Passy, Sept. 12. 79
Mr Franklin presents his Compliments to Mr Lee, and sends all the Journals he has; but, as he has not yet had time to read them, he prays Mr Lee to return them when he has perus’d them.—
 
Addressed: The honble A Lee Esqr / Paris.
Notation: Sepr. 12. 1779
