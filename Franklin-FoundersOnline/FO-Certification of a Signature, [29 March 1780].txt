[March 29, 1780]
I do hereby certify that the Signature P Henry to this Instrument is truly the Hand-writing of the Governor of Virginia. At Passy, this 29th Day of March 1780.
Minister Plenipotentiary OF THE United StatesAT THE Court OF France
