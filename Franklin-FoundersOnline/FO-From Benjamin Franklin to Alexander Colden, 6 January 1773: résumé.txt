 Title: FO-From Benjamin Franklin to Alexander Colden, 6 January 1773: résumé
Date: 1773-01-06
Author: Franklin, Benjamin

<London, January 6, 1773. Returns two protested bills, Zeph[aniah] Turner on Barnes & Ridgate for £72 7s. 5d. and William Taylor on Perkins, Buchanan & Brown for £15; the charges on each are 5s. 9d. Asks to have the bills acknowledged and to be credited with £87 18s. 11d.>
