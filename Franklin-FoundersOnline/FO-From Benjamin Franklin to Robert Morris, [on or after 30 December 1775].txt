[On or after December 30, 1775]
Mr. Franklin presents his Compliments to Mr. Morris, and not knowing what was done by the Committee with regard to the other Prisoners, requests Mr. Morris would direct what is to be done with these.
 
Addressed: To / Robt Morris Esqr
