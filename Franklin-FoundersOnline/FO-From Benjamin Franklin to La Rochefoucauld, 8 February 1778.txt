Sunday, Feb. 8. [1778]
M. Franklin presents his Respects to M. le Duc de Rochefoucauld, and will, with his Grandson, have the honour of Dining with Made. la Duchesse dâ€™Enville on Monday the 16th. agreable to her obliging Invitation. He will also mention it to Mr. Deane.
M. Franklin will expect with Pleasure, the Honour intended him by M. le Duc, of taking a Breakfast with him to morrow morning.
