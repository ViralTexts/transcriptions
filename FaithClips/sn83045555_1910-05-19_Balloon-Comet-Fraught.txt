St. Louis, May 19 – Fraught with mundane excitement but not eventful astronomically the balloon expedition to explore the tail of Halley’s comet, which began here last night at 6:35 o’clock ended near Hillview, Greene country, Ill at 11:20 o’clock.  The aerostat travelled 65 miles.
